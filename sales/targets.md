## WHERE TO FIND CONTACTS

### LinkedIn (best for all tiers)
Search: `"[Company name]" AND ("VP" OR "Director" OR "Head of") AND ("digital" OR "innovation" OR "product" OR "clinical")`

### Email format guessing
Most companies use: `firstname.lastname@company.com` or `flastname@company.com`
Verify with: hunter.io (free tier) or rocketreach.co

### Conferences to attend (in-person = 10x email)
- **SXSW Health** (March, Austin) — innovation buyers
- **ViVE** (Feb, Nashville) — digital health buyers
- **HLTH** (October, Las Vegas) — health insurance + tech
- **SHRM Annual** (June) — HR/benefits buyers
- **InsureTech Connect** (October, Las Vegas) — insurance innovation
